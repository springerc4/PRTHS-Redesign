The Chicago Terminal Chapter meets quarterly in the Pullman Visitor Center in Historic Pullman, Illinois. The center is located at 11141 South Cottage Grove Avenue four blocks west of I-94 at the 111th St. exit. Meetings run from 2:00PM until 5:00PM on Saturdays four times per year. In addition outings have been planned as members can arrange schedules. We have a number of experts in the chapter who are always willing to provide help and inspiration to anyone. We have two former tower operators at Englewood, the former track Superintendent for the Chicago Division, A signal maintainer and several crewmen that were directly employed by the PRR. In addition we have had over sixty people who have attended meetings to date. Since meeting dates are set at the prior meeting for the latest information please contact Marv Cadwell at: ld_mlc43@comcast.net

The New England Chapter serves members in all six New England states. Official membership meetings are held quarterly in different locations in the region to "share the distance". The Chapter sponsors additional activities such as home layout visits, group railfan trips, and other events between meetings. The quarterly newsletter, The East Wind, covers a wide variety of topics including club news and schedules, modeling stories, photos, memorabilia, Pennsy history, and more -all with a Pennsy flavor.

Annual dues are $15 payable by January 1. Dues cover mailing and copying expenses of the club. Anyone living in or traveling through New England is invited to come to our meetings and check us out. Contact Tim Garner at t.a.garner@verizon.net for the latest meeting information.

The N.C. Chapter meets the second Sunday of February, April, September and November each year at Otterbein United Methodist Church, 301 W. Philadelphia Street, York, Pa. The meetings consists of a short business meeting and some type of program or presentation about the PRR and related subjects. A SIGNAL BRIDGE newsletter is sent out about two weeks prior to each meeting to keep Chapter members updated on items of interest concerning the Chapter, local railroad news, and national PRR T&HS information. N.C. Chapter dues are $10.00 a year.
